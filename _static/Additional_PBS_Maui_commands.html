Additional PBS/Maui commands


Following are some useful commands that will help you monitor the available resources and your jobs:

    
	
  1. showq : Detailed listing of running jobs, much like qstat.
    2. 
	
  2. qhost : Show the status of nodes, queues, jobs. (not accurate) 
    3. 
	
  3. showbf : Shows resources available for immediate use.
    4. 
	
  4. showstart jobid : Shows estimates of when job can/will start.
    5. 
	
  5. qdel -p jobid : Will force purge the job if it is not killed by qdel. 
    6. 
	
  6. checkjob jobid : Will list job details. 
    7. 
	
  7. qstat -f jobid : List full information known about jobid. 
    8. 
	
  8. qalter : Alter the parameters of a job. Usually applies to a job that is in the Q state and you want to change values (like cpu-time, target nodes) before it runs. 
    9. 
	
  9. qhold : Put a hold on a job before it starts running (applies to job in Q state). 
    10. 
	
  10. qrls : Release hold on pbs batch jobs. 
    11. 
	
  11. tracejob jobid : Using it can help identify where, how, a why a job failed. 
    12.
